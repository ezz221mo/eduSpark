About Me

Hello! I'm Alex, a former full-stack developer turned educator. I believe that coding is the new literacy and every child should understand the logic behind the technology they use every day.


My teaching style is project-based and interactive. We won't just memorize syntax — we'll build games, websites, and solve real-world problems. I have over 10 years of experience in the tech industry working for companies like Google.
